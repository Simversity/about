Front End Engineer

Description

We are looking for good & capable Front End Engineers who understand the power and importance of JavaScript and its role in defining the most awesome products on the web.

While more and more focus is shifting towards Mobile applications, our web product is an ever-important part of what we are and what we do.

Job Responsibility
  • You will be working closely with our UI and Backend teams to continually improve our web product.
  • You do not have to bother about Internet Explorer 6,7 & 8. We do not support them.
  • You will be responsible for the website's performance and compatibility with all modern web browsers.
  • You will help us maintain and constantly improve the huge JavaScript code behind our front-end.
  • You will keep shipping code, we will keep deploying it.
Requirements
  • Experience of building interactive & functional websites using Javascript.
  • Solid understanding of how JavaScript interacts with HTML and CSS.
  • An ability to read that code and understand what it does.
  • An ability to debug, break & fix, event/callback based Javascript code.
  • Experience and familiarity with Python is a plus, but not required.
  • Comfortable with an agile approach that takes user-feedback as paramount and happily revises work based on that input
  • Location: Pune
